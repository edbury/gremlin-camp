*After-Action Report: mission-01-aar.md*

- **Mission:** Another Bug Hunt
- **Roster:**

    - Naima Kinsler (Marine)

- **Locations:**
    - Samsa system
    - Samsa VI (Class-4 environment, tropical storms, dense jungle)
    - Key sites:

    - Crew currently en route to Prospero's Dream
