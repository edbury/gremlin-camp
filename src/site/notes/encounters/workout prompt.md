## Workout routine Chat GPT prompt

```
I would like help putting together a daily workout routine.

**Information about myself:**
- Male
- 220lbs
- 5'8" tall
- 37 years old
- No health concerns or injuries.

**Goals:**
- Lose weight. I'd like to weigh around 160lbs.
- Improve mobility and cardiovascular fitness.

**Restrictions:**
- No equipment available.
- 30 minutes to 60 minutes a day.
- Format the entire workout routine in markdown. 
- Prefer yoga for mobility exercises, include a traditional sun salutation.
- Include instructions and suggested times for cardiovascular exercises.
- Include instructions and recommended reps for bodyweight strength training exercises.
- Include instructions and recommendations for yoga posts.
```
